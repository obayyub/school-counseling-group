GAP YEAR

More students are seeking discrete structured experiences as alternatives to college (or even high school). These experiences are most often taken in "gaps" before, during or after college but can take place anytime.

Our approach is to work collaboratively with gap year candidates, believing that, whenever possible, the best results occur with the fullest personal investment and involvement of the students themselves. Gap experiences run the gamut from living at home and working, to internships or pursuing alternative study to year-long travel/work options spanning the globe.

Here are a few of the projects we have recently supported: culinary arts in Paris, service work in Africa and South America, a business internship in Australia, travel and medication in India, documentary film production in Texas, language study in Spain and France, a NOLS semester in Patagonia, a pilgrimage on the El Camino Santiago in Spain, volunteering and working in the DC area.

Our services are designed for the motivated student who wants a structured, guided, collaborative process or for the less motivated student in need of direction. In either case, our goal is that the process of identifying and selecting a gap experience is personally enlightening, as well as practical and productive.
