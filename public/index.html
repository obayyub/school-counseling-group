We offer comprehensive and dynamic education consulting services in the greater Washington area

The School Counseling Group is a small group of professionals dedicated to providing outstanding service for clients. Collectively, our consultants have spent decades in a variety of settings as teachers, administrators, counselors and coaches. Our uniqueness as a firm is our capacity to collaborate, formally and informally, as a diverse team of counselors operating together under one roof and with a shared sense of purpose.

Our Mission

SCG consultants bring wisdom, experience, common sense and compassion to our mission of serving families in need of expert guidance on educational options, emotional/behavioral challenges and/or placement in colleges, universities and gap year programs; day, boarding, and post-graduate schools; therapeutic and "special needs" schools or programs.
